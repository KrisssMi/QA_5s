Environment:

Test cases:

Test ID: 1

Title: Registration with email and password

Pre-requirements:

  1. Opened registration page: https://www.lcwaikiki.by/ru-RU/BY/register
  1. Had email service : (You can use temp-email: https://temp-mail.io/ru )
StepExpected result
Enter your phone number in the appropriate fieldThe entered phone number is displayed
Enter First Name in "Имя" fieldIn the "Имя" field the entered First Name is displayed
Enter Last Name in "Фамилия" fieldIn the "Фамилия" field the entered Last Name is displayed
Enter email in "Электронная почта" fieldIn the "Электронная почта" field the entered e-mail is displayed
Choose gender: Female/MaleCheckbox “Пол" should be checked
Click on checkbox in "Подписаться на новости”Checkbox “Подписаться на новости" should be checked
Click on button "Зарегистрироваться”You should see next text on the page "Ваша регистрация успешно выполнена.”

Test ID: 2

Title: Skirt search

Pre-requirements:

  1. Opened main page: https://conteshop.by/
StepExpected result
At the top of the site click on the "Искать по сайту" fieldFocused on this field
Enter "Юбка" in "Поиск" fieldIn the "Искать по сайту" field the entered text is displayed
Click "Найти" button to the right of "Найти" fieldOpened the page with the results of the search for "Юбка”

Test ID: 3

Title: Adding skirt with size M to the bucket

Pre-requirements:

1. Opened any t-shirt page: https://conteshop.by/ru/catalogsearch/result?q=Юбка

StepExpected result
Select size M in the sizes sectionLetter M should be selected and highlighted in a black frame
Specify the quantity of the product (click on "+", in case of adding one more thing)When adding the quantity of the product automatically changes
Click "Добавить в корзину" button under size sectionA pop-up window appears that the product has been added to the cart
Click on the icon of Bag in the header sectionThe shopping cart page opened with chose skirt M-size in it

Verdict: All tests passed.
